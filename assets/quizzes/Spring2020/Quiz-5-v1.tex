\problem{6} The amount of water in a tank $t$ minutes after it has started to drain is given by 

$W = 10(t-15)^2$ gal.  Be sure to include proper units in your answers.

\begin{subproblems}

\item How many gallons of water are in the tank at time $t=0$? \vfill
\item At what rate is the water running out at the end of 5 minutes? \vfill
\item What is the average rate at which the water flows out during the first 5 minutes? \vfill

\end{subproblems}
